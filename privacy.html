# Privacy Policy for Eagles of Space **Last Updated:** May 03, 2026 DeathSword Interactive ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. Information We Collect ### 1.1 Leaderboard Data When you play the Game, you may choose a pilot nickname. This nickname, along with your high score, country (derived from your device locale), and difficulty level, is stored in our database (Firebase) to display on the global leaderboard. We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal contact information. ### 1.2 Advertising Data The Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use certain data (such as your device's advertising ID and location data) to serve personalized ads. For more information, please visit [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy). ## 2. How We Use Your Information We use the collected data solely for: - Displaying global high scores. - Improving Game performance and user experience. - Serving relevant advertisements through AdMob. ## 3. Data Storage and Security Your leaderboard data is stored on Firebase (a Google service). We take reasonable measures to protect this data. However, please be aware that no security measure is perfect or impenetrable. ## 4. Children's Privacy Our Game is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. ## 5. Your Choices You can change your pilot nickname or reset your local game data at any time through the in-game settings menu. ## 6.

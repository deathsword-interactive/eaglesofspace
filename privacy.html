Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how What Should I Watch? (the "Application") handles data. The Application is developed by DeathSword Interactive.

1. Information Collection

Personal Data: The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information. We do not require account registration.

Third-Party Services: The Application uses Google AdMob for advertisements. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers for advertising personalization and analytics. You can manage your ad settings through your device's operating system.

2. App Usage Data

Your local search history and watchlist are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

3. Children's Privacy

The Application does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13.

4. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us via deathsword-interactive.github.io
